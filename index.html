Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

How does the dedicated editor model work?

After the test edit, you are matched with an editor who understands your style. You send raw footage and references, then receive edited videos through your preferred communication channel.

What's the turnaround time for videos?

Most videos are delivered within 24-72 hours depending on length, complexity, revisions and footage quality.

Do I own the final videos?

Yes. Once completed, the final edited videos are yours to publish on your social channels, ads or website.

What if I don't like the edit?

You can request revisions so the edit matches your brand, pacing, hook style, captions, music and overall direction.
